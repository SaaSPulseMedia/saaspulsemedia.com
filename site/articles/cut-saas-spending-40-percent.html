How to Cut Your SaaS Spending by 40% Without Losing Productivity

Learn how to audit and reduce your SaaS subscriptions.

✍ SaaS Pulse Media 📅 14 February 2026 ⏱ 1 min read

The Hidden Cost of SaaS Sprawl

The average company wastes $500-$1,500 per month on unused subscriptions.

Step 1: The SaaS Audit

  1. Check credit card statements
  2. Survey team members
  3. Review app store subscriptions

Step 2: Categorize and Prioritize

  • Essential: Cannot operate without
  • Nice-to-have: Helpful but not critical
  • Unused: No one touched in 30+ days

Step 3: Cut and Consolidate

  • Cancel unused tools immediately
  • Downgrade plans you are not fully using
  • Switch to annual billing for 20-40% savings
